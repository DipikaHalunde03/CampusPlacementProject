A guide to interview
preparation for Students

Interview preparation is vital for effectively evaluating
candidates, promoting the employer brand, and enhancing
candidate experience. It involves crafting questions,
understanding candidates, preparing responses, coordinating
with interviewers, managing time, and honing selling skills.
Proper preparation facilitates better hiring and
a streamlined process.
When it comes to interviews, it’s good to ‘be prepared.’


TECHNICAL INTERVIEW PREPARATION

Technical interviews are a part of the hiring process for technical professionals: it's a highly focused and rigorous process that assesses your technical skills, personality, and problem-solving abilities. In the same vein as "show, don't tell," you need to demonstrate your skills to the interviewer, rather than just stating them

DOMAIN SPECIFIC INTERVIEW PREPARATION
